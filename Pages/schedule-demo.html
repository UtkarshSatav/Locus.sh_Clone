How can Locus help Manage Your Logistics?

  • Locus' proprietary geocoding engine converts the fuzziest of the addresses into precise geographical coordinates thereby helping your on-ground executives locate addresses easily.
  • Digitize all your operational variables such as fleets, delivery persons etc. to come up with the best route plan every day.
  • Track your orders in real-time with the Locus Live Dashboard. Locus' all-mile delivery app Locus On The Road (LOTR) helps delivery partners process orders.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Locus' pricing structured?

At Locus, we tailor our pricing to align with each client's unique needs. Our flexible service options are designed to adapt to the specific requirements and scale of your operations, ensuring you receive value-driven solutions.

Is there a minimum or maximum pricing for Locus's services?

We do not have fixed subscription fees; our pricing is based on the volume of shipments processed through our platform. This approach allows us to offer cost-effective solutions that scale with your business.

Can I subscribe to only specific modules of Locus's services?

Absolutely! You can choose to subscribe to individual modules that meet your specific needs. We also offer comprehensive packages that combine multiple features for added value. To explore the best options for your business, please connect with our sales team.

Does Locus offer customized solutions for unique business requirements?

Yes, we understand that each business has distinct needs. If you require a feature not currently available, we encourage you to discuss it with our sales team. We are committed to developing solutions that provide significant value to your operations.

How does Locus handle customer data?

We prioritize data security and adhere to strict policies to protect your customer information. Our data security measures are among the best in the industry, trusted by leading companies worldwide.
