Transporter Management

Automate contract processes and optimize costs in real-time, and gaining a strategic advantage for dispatch planning and carrier selection.

Get In Touch Watch Demos

Optimize transporter contract life cycle with intelligent automation

The Transporter management capability is designed to streamline and enhance outsourced fulfillment across transporter networks. The application enables shippers and transporters to have visibility on quotes,leverage intelligent order allocation, and drive friction-free reconciliations. Through a mobile-friendly portal, transporters can get quick and hassle-free payouts on invoices, submit quotes to businesses, and easily check status updates on current orders.

Gain end-to-end visibility into transporter operations

Centralize transporter operations on a single, easy-to-use dashboard for powering cost-effective outsourced fulfillment and real-time visibility of transporter activities. Digitally onboard, track, and renew contracts on a centralized repository and create custom flows to automate all phases of a contract lifecycle.

Expedite transporter payments with frictionless invoice reconciliations

Stay on top of invoice reconciliations with system-verified costs based on planned & on-ground execution data. Enable swift reconciliations with transporters via automated invoices, intelligent alerts for compliance breaches, & shared business adherences.

Manage transporters at scale

Automatically select the best-suited transporters for every order across multiple legs of a shipping journey. Receive competitive rates for your planned trips from multiple transporters via the Locus-powered trip bidding platform.

Allocate orders to the right transporters via rule-based engine

Auto-dispatch orders with the right transporter based on cost, speed, business zones, contract based commitments, transporter performance & other business constraints.

Fuel positive unit economics with volume-driven transporter utilization

Streamline shipping operations by flexibly assigning orders with transporters that match precise capacity & demand requirements, to drive cost efficiency without compromising on SLAs.
